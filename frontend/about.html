Our Mission

Athletes today have access to incredible tracking technology, but managing data across multiple platforms creates complexity and confusion. Should you train hard today or prioritize recovery? What does your HRV trend really mean when combined with your training load and sleep quality?

Athlytx solves this. We unify data from your devices into a single, actionable readiness score that answers the most important question every athlete asks: "How ready am I to perform today?"

The Problem: Multiple devices, fragmented insights, decision paralysis.
Our Solution: One unified score (0-100) that combines recovery, sleep, strain, and HRV trends into clear, actionable guidance.

The Athlytx Score

Our proprietary algorithm combines data from all your devices into a single 0-100 readiness score that answers one critical question: "How ready am I to perform today?"

Key Components

Recovery
Body's recovery from training stress
Sleep Quality
Duration, efficiency, and consistency
Training Load
Recent workout intensity and volume
Vital Signs
HRV, heart rate, and stress markers

Score Interpretation

80-100
Peak condition - ready for intense training
60-79
Ready to train - moderate to high intensity
40-59
Light training - focus on recovery
0-39
Rest day - prioritize recovery
Smart Confidence Indicators: Every score includes a confidence level based on data quality and completeness, so you always know how much to trust your readiness assessment.

Why Athlytx?

For Data-Driven Athletes

You've invested in premium tracking technology. Strava knows your training, Garmin tracks your wellness, Oura understands your sleep, Whoop monitors your strain. Each platform excels in its domain—but the real insights emerge when they work together. Athlytx is that missing layer of intelligence.

One Score, Infinite Insights

Stop juggling multiple apps. Wake up, check your Athlytx Score, and instantly know: "Am I ready to push hard today, or should I focus on recovery?" Our algorithm does the heavy lifting, so you can focus on training and performing.

Science Meets Simplicity

Our scoring methodology is grounded in sports science—HRV trends, training stress balance (CTL/ATL/TSB), sleep architecture, recovery metrics—but presented in an instantly actionable format. Complexity under the hood, clarity on the surface.

Built by Athletes, For Athletes: Athlytx was created by someone who lived the problem—drowning in data, thirsting for insights. We respect your individual devices while creating something greater than the sum of its parts.
